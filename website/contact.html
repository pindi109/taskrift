Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Creating a project in Taskrift takes just a few taps. From the sidebar, tap "+ New Project", give it a name, and choose a colour. Once created, any new task can be assigned to that project from the task creation screen. You can also move existing tasks between projects by tapping the task and editing its project field. Projects are colour-coded so you can identify them at a glance across your task list.
No — your tasks and projects are securely stored in the cloud via Firebase and linked to your account. When you reinstall Taskrift and log back in with the same account, everything will sync back automatically. We recommend always using an account rather than continuing as a guest, as guest data is stored locally only and may be lost on uninstall or device change.
When you set a due date on a task, Taskrift can send you a push notification reminder. By default, you'll get a reminder on the morning of the due date. You can customise when reminders fire (e.g., 1 day before, 2 hours before) in your account settings under Notifications. To receive reminders, you must grant Taskrift notification permissions in your device settings. You can opt out at any time.
