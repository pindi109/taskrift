Taskrift is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only what we need to provide the service, and we never sell your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what rights you have under UK/EU GDPR.

1. Who We Are

Taskrift ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for the Taskrift mobile application and associated web services. The Taskrift app is a task management tool enabling users to create tasks, set due dates, mark tasks complete, and organise work into projects.

For data protection enquiries, contact us at: privacy@taskrift.app

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Task & project data: Task titles, descriptions, due dates, completion status, project names, and any labels or priorities you assign.
  • User-generated content: Any notes or comments you add to tasks.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data: Features used, screens viewed, session duration, and crash reports to improve the app experience.
  • Authentication tokens: Securely stored tokens to keep you logged in.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment card details (processed by third-party payment providers).
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts.
  • We do not read any data outside the Taskrift app.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery: To create your account, store your tasks and projects, and sync data across your devices.
  • App improvement: To analyse usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Notifications: To send due date reminders you have opted into.
  • Customer support: To respond to your queries and resolve issues.
  • Legal compliance: To meet our obligations under applicable law.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing your account and task data is necessary to deliver the Taskrift service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Analytics and security monitoring to maintain and improve the app.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Push notification reminders — you may withdraw consent at any time in your device settings.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where we are required by law to retain or disclose data.

5. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate Taskrift. Each is bound by its own privacy policy and appropriate data processing agreements:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Functions): We use Firebase for user authentication, real-time database storage of your tasks and projects, and serverless functions. Firebase is operated by Google LLC under the Google Cloud Privacy Notice. Data may be stored on servers in the EU or US (subject to Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting to identify and fix bugs. Reports contain device information and stack traces but no personal task content.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Anonymised usage analytics. We have configured IP anonymisation and do not share identifiable data with Google for advertising purposes.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: App distribution. Their platforms may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes. We will disclose data only if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Cookies & Tracking

The Taskrift mobile app does not use cookies. If you access our website (taskrift.app), we may use the following:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Session management — no consent required.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics with IP anonymisation — consent requested via cookie banner.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings or via our cookie consent banner.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, we permanently delete all task and project data within 30 days.
  • Backups: Encrypted backups may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion for disaster recovery purposes.
  • Legal obligations: Some data may be retained longer where required by law.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting database access to authenticated users only.
  • Data encrypted at rest by Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Regular security reviews and dependency audits.

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@taskrift.app.

9. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 13–22)

If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing (Art. 18): Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making (Art. 22): We do not engage in solely automated decision-making with legal effects.

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@taskrift.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

10. Children's Privacy

Taskrift is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in EEA countries where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us at privacy@taskrift.app and we will delete it promptly.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK/EEA, including the United States (where Google/Firebase infrastructure operates). Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and reliance on the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA).

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notification at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of Taskrift after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact & Data Requests

For any privacy-related questions, data access requests, or complaints:

  • Email: privacy@taskrift.app
  • Response time: Within 30 days of receipt
  • Postal address: Taskrift, c/o Data Controller, United Kingdom

For urgent security concerns: security@taskrift.app
